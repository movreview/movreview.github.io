Madaari is a 2016 Indian social-thriller film directed by Nishikant Kamat,Assistantly Directed By Nishit Mehta, presented by Bharat Shah and Nishant Pitti produced by Shailesh Singh, Madan Paliwal, Sutapa Sikdar and Shailja Kejriwal and co-produced by Nishant Pitti & Kshitiz Bhardwaj. The film stars Irrfan Khan, Vishesh Bansal, Jimmy Shergill, Tushar Dalvi and Nitesh Pandey and was released on 22 July 2016.
